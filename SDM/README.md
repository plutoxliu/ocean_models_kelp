# Species Distribution Model for *Macrocystis* in the Southern Hemisphere
## Occurance records 
Contain *M. "pyrifera"*, *M. "integrifolia"* and *M. "angustifolia"*
<br><br>
## Environmental variables
### From Bio-ORACLE database: https://www.bio-oracle.org/index.php
- Physical ocean variables: sea surface temperatures, salinity, sea water velocity, pH
- Nutrients: nitrate, phosphate, silicate, dissolved molecular oxygen, iron
- Atmospheric variables: photosynthetic available radiation
- Sea ice cover
<br><br>
### From other database
#### WorldClim2: https://www.worldclim.org/
- Air temperature data: maximum of the warmest month, mean annual, minimum of the coldest month
#### ERA5: https://cds.climate.copernicus.eu/datasets/reanalysis-era5-single-levels-monthly-means?tab=overview
- Wind fetch calculated from wind direction

- Tidal amplitude

- High resoltion mean significant wave height
#### CMEMS: https://data.marine.copernicus.eu/product/GLOBAL_MULTIYEAR_PHY_001_030/description
- Mean significant wave height not covered by Passaro et al. (2021)
